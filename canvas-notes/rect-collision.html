Rectangle Collision

Two rectangles will collide if the following are ALL true:

  • The left edge of Rectangle 1 is less than the right edge of Rectangle 2,
  • The right edge of Rectangle 1 is greater than the left edge of Rectangle 2,
  • The bottom edge of Rectangle 1 is greater than the top edge of Rectangle 2,
  • The top edge of Rectangle 1 is less than the bottom edge of Rectangle 2.
